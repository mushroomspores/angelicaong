Hidden Library #2: Unbound Narrative
18 Jan – 10 Mar 2025
FLOOR_, Seoul, Korea

Hidden Library #2: Unbound Narrative is the second edition of FLOOR_'s Hidden Library exhibition series comprising work by Angelica Ong, Yi-Hsuan Huang, Jeong Hur, Dain Kim, Jade Lien, Chienyu Liu, Song Lu, Marooso, Parker McComb, Xin Lui Ng, and Sui Roh.

In this show, I presented 6 artist books—《起伏》sea tiles: dreaming tides, gathering and unravelling (2024), the smallest registers of motion《微尘似梦》｢上り月や 手に震える｣ (2024), ｢霜の声｣ heavenly bodies (2023),《天体》heavenly bodies ｢霜の声｣ l'équinoxe (2023), ｢朧月｣ a resplendent afterimage (2022-23), and on things fugitive《如朝露暮霭》｢果敢無い物事｣ sur les choses fugitives (2022)—and excerpts from my long-term photo series Untitled (a stray poem) (2020-ongoing), which were presented alongside a selection of words and phrases that were installed as white vinyl text adhered directly to the white walls.
