a whisper and a silhouette. 2020. Handbound. 20 x 14 x 0.5 cm. (7.87 x 5.51 x 0.20 in). Fabriano multipaper, tracing paper, and mounting board.

a whisper and a silhouette is my first handbound photo book. It is flat-back case-bound book and contains digital inkjet prints of images shot on 35mm film as well as text in English, Chinese, and Japanese.
